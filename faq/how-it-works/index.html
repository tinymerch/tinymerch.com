TinyMerch uses a single embed script to give you a fully functional store

You'll simply upload a design for a shirt or poster in your TinyMerch Dashboard, configure a few simple options, such as color, size, etc,. Then you decide how much to sell your products for, and that's pretty much it. Add the script to your site and never worry about it again! No maintence, inventory, worrying about order fulfillment. We take care of it all through our trusted vendor network.
