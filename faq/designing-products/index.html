Easily upload your custom designs on selected products.

From your TinyMerch Dashboard you can easily add products to your store! We use DTG printing for fabric products, so avoid PNG files that include semi-transparent values. After uploading and placing your image, TinyMerch will generate high-resolution print files automatically.

Finally, name your price! You can choose any profit amount above the base item's list price. Is it a $15 print? $50? $500? That's up to you! (And what your customers can afford 😜)
